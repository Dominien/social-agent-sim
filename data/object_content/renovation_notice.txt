MODERNIZATION ANNOUNCEMENT pursuant to §559 BGB

Dear tenants,

We hereby announce the following modernization measures:

1. Energy-efficient renovation of the building envelope (facade insulation, window replacement)
2. Replacement of the heating system (conversion to heat pump)
3. Modernization of sanitary facilities in all residential units
4. Redesign of the stairwell and entrance area
5. Conversion of the top floor

Estimated construction start: in 8 weeks
Estimated construction period: 12 months

The modernization costs will be proportionally allocated to rent.
The estimated rent increase is 45-60% of the current base rent.
